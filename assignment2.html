Economics

Economics is a social science that focuses on the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. The study of economics is primarily concerned with analyzing the choices that individuals, businesses, governments, and nations make to allocate limited resources.

Management

Management is how businesses organize and direct workflow, operations, and employees to meet company goals. The primary goal of management is to create an environment that lets employees work efficiently and productively.

Marketing

Marketing is defined as both "the process or technique of promoting, selling, and distributing a product or service," and "an aggregate of functions involved in moving goods from producer to consumer.
